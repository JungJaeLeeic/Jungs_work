In addition to eigenvectors and eigenvalues, other unique properties of matrices and vectors can be visualised geometrically. Commutation is a shared property of two matrices defined by: $$ A \dot B = B \dot A $$ Geometrically speaking, this means that applying the spacial transformations A then B is identical to applying the transformation B then A.

△: A → B

▲: B → A
